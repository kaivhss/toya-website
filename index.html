An unofficial wiki written in dedication to Aoyagi Toya showcasing his character

"I want to surpass that night... and witness a scenery I've never seen before with my own eyes."

Toya's father was a famous classical musician and trained Toya and his two older brothers in classical music from a very young age, specifically in piano and violin. His father would isolate Toya and force him to study classical music at all hours of the day. This caused him to despise his father, and by association, the entirety of classical music. As such, he started performing street music to rebel against his father and eventually met Akito Shinonome, a hardworking and passionate musician, while singing on the street.

He sang together with Akito under the name BAD DOGS before forming Vivid BAD SQUAD with An Shiraishi, daughter of the legendary street singer Ken Shiraishi, and Kohane Azusawa, a newbie muscian looking to improve her confidence. Even after joining Vivid BAD SQUAD, he still operates under the BAD DOGS unit with Akito.

Personality

Toya is quiet and calm but has a strong mind. His face is often deadpan, and people other than Akito Shinonome and Tsukasa Tenma (his close family friend) have difficulty reading his expressions. When people talk to him, they usually mistake him for being rude, as he is blunt and often misphrases his statements. Until becoming part of Vivid BAD SQUAD, he wasn't one to smile frequently. Despite his stoic demeanor, he is kind and quick to help others. He can occasionally be airheaded or gullible.

He is good at studying and gets high grades, a trait that he shares with Kohane Azusawa. He can sometimes be strict with Akito about the latter neglecting his studies.

Appearance

Toya has sharp grey eyes and a mole under the corner of his left eye. His hair is short and bicolored, the right side a dark blue, the left a light blue. His voice is cool and calm.
